About Us

ARM is a household appliance repair and maintenance provider in the north suburbs of the Chicagoland area. We service washers and dryers, dishwashers, refrigerators and window a/c units. We offer a variety of services including cleanings, hardware replacement, preventative maintenance, and Freon recharge. On top of that we also have various refurbished appliances available for purchase - limited warranty included. If you have any old appliances you would like to sell or just simply want hauled away, let us know - we offer these services on a case-by-case basis.
